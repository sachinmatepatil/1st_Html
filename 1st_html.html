A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Here's the Achievement of Kalam Sir

1. AEROSPACE ENGINEER : From selling newspaper at railway stations, he went on to study Aerospace Engineering from MADRAS INSTITUTE of TECHNOLOGY.

2. MISSILE MAN of INDIA : Dr. Kalam played a major role in developing missiles AGNI ( an intermediate range ballistic missile ) and PRITHVI ( surface-to-surface missile ) for India. Since then, he became the missile man of India .

3. The BHARAT RATNA : Dr. Kalam received Padma Bhusan in 1981, Padma Vibhusan in 1990 and Indias highest civilian honour- The Bharat Ratna in 1997 for his excellent service in field of science & technology.

"Failure will never overtake me if my determination to succeed is strong enough.- A.P.J. Abdul Kalam "
